How do I become an informed voter?


It can be tricky to be an informed voter. You have to be able to see past ads and rehtoric, but it can be done. Be sure to research thoroughly before making an opinion. Don't just believe what you hear on tv or through social media. By doing these things you can be an informed voter and you can make a positive impact on your community. Great job on taking a first step towards being a responsible citizen! Below are some activities you can do to improve your informed voting skills

Thinking Activities


Watch the following video by the YouTube channel Crash Course and then answer the questions:




1. Were you surprised by any of the three main characters that voters use to make their decision?
2. Does your parents or family's opinions influence your own opinions?
3. Were you surprised by any of the characteristics that people use to make their opinions off of? Do any of them seem unfair or unjust?
4. Do you think we need politicians that better represent the "typical" American?
5. Was there anything else you were suprised about?
